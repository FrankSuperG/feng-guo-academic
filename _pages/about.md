I am an **FWO Senior Postdoctoral Fellow** and **IEEE Senior Member**, currently working as a **joint postdoctoral researcher at VITO and UHasselt (Belgium)**.

My work combines **electrochemical battery modeling**, **AI**, and **control theory** to build reliable estimation and fault-tolerant control methods for next-generation battery systems.

I develop physics-informed and deployment-oriented methods that bridge model fidelity, computational efficiency, and real-world BMS robustness.

My current focus is physics-informed modeling, robust state estimation, and scalable methods for cloud-enabled battery management.

I am open to collaborations on electrochemical battery modeling, state estimation, and AI-enhanced battery management systems.

### Research Interests
- Electrochemical Battery Modeling and Simulation
- State Estimation and Control of Energy Storage Systems
- Battery Fault Diagnosis and Health Management
- Artificial Intelligence for Battery Systems (AI4Batteries)
